About

Inside the Mind

Built by Ahmed ur Rehman, Haseeb Haroon, Abdurrahman Shahid & Muhammad Mehdi, a group of developers from IoBM. IntelliQ runs on a transformer-based language model with a 128K context window, designed to reason through problems and hold long conversations without losing track.

The more context you give it, the better it performs. Still being improved, so feedback is always welcome.
